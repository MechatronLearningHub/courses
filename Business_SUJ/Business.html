About Our Courses For School Students

Business & Entrepreneurship Skills

Career Awareness & Innovation Program for Class 5+ by Mechatron Learning Hub.

Future & Career Readiness is a unique, real-world skills program offered by Mechatron Learning Hub for students from Class 5 onwards. This immersive course helps children explore future careers, experience entrepreneurship, and solve real-life problems through guided challenges — preparing them to dream big and act smart in the real world.

  • Target Group: Students from Class 5 & above.
  • Offered By: Mechatron Learning Hub
  • Course Duration: 3 interactive modules (05 hours each) = 15 hours
  • Format: Online / Offline (in‑campus)
  • Includes: Group projects, creativity workshops & skill-based certificates
  • Goal: Develop career awareness, entrepreneurial mindset & innovation skills

What are you going to learn?

Module 1: Career Discovery Camp – Explore the World of Work

Students explore exciting future careers through interactive role-plays, games, and virtual job stations.
Activities: Career simulations, interest mapping, day-in-the-life videos
Course Duration: 05 hours


Course Overview:
The Career Discovery Camp is an interactive and engaging course designed to help students explore potential career paths. Through a series of modules, participants will gain insights into various industries, career roles, and the skills required to succeed in them. The course is designed to inspire and inform students about future career opportunities while helping them identify their passions and strengths.

Module 2: Mini CEOs Program – Entrepreneurship for Young Minds

An exciting introduction to how businesses are born! Students go from idea to pitch with basics of business planning, budgeting, branding, and public speaking.
Activities: Business idea creation, logo design, pitching their brand
Course Duration: 05 hours


Course Overview:
The Mini CEOs Program is an introductory course designed for aspiring young entrepreneurs to understand the basics of entrepreneurship. The course covers essential concepts like ideation, building a business plan, understanding basic finance, and crafting a creative pitch presentation. It equips participants with the foundational skills needed to start a business and gives them the confidence to present their ideas effectively.

Module 03: Innovation Challenge – Build to Solve, Present to Lead

Students participate in a mini hackathon where they identify real problems (in schools, society, or environment) and brainstorm creative solutions.
Activities: Group ideation, design thinking, pitch presentation Course Duration: 05 hours


Course Overview:
The Innovation Challenge is a dynamic, hands-on experience where school students identify real-world problems (social or school-based), brainstorm creative solutions, and present their ideas through prototypes and pitches. This challenge introduces the fundamentals of design thinking, team collaboration, and public speaking while encouraging empathy and social innovation.
