About Our Courses for College Students

Future Ready – Innovative Tech Skills for Young Minds

Innovative Tech Skills for Young Minds is a future‑focused, hands‑on course offered by Mechatron Learning Hub for school students in Class 8 and above. From drag‑and‑drop coding and robotics to AI, design, and STEM innovation—this course introduces children to the technologies shaping our world, through fun projects, real‑life applications, and interactive tools.

Course Snapshot

  • Target Group: Class 8 to 12
  • Offered By: Mechatron Learning Hub
  • Duration: 06 modules × 08 hours each = 48 Hours
  • Format: Online / Offline Workshops (in‑campus)
  • Certificate: Tech Skills Certificate by Mechatron
  • Ideal For: Students curious about coding, robotics, AI, design & innovation
