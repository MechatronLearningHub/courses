About Our Courses For School Students

Innovative Technical Skills

Innovative Tech Skills for Young Mind is a future‑focused, hands‑on course offered by Mechatron Learning Hub for school students in Class 8 and above. What are you going to learn?

Module 1: CodePlay – Easy Coding for Beginners

Get started with fun, interactive drag-and-drop coding using Scratch, then move to basic Python with real-world mini programs.
Activities: Create games, animations, and beginner Python apps
Course Duration: 8 hours


Course Overview:
The CodePlay course introduces kids to the world of coding, starting with basic concepts and progressing to beginner-friendly programming using Scratch and Python.
The course begins with the fun and interactive world of drag-and-drop coding with Scratch, where students can create their own games, stories, and animations. Once students are comfortable, they will transition to learning Python basics, one of the most popular programming languages, to start writing simple code.

Module 2: Autonomous Robotics – Build Smart Machines

Hands-on robotics for young minds! Learn to build and control basic robots with sensors and logic.
Activities: Line-following robot, obstacle detection, robotics logic building.
Course Duration: 8 hours


Course Overview:
The Autonomous Robotics course introduces children to the world of robotics while focusing on the development of emotional intelligence, self-awareness, and empathy. Using real-life scenarios and interactive robotics projects, students will learn how robots can be programmed to understand and respond to emotions. By exploring how robots can simulate human-like behavior and emotions, kids will develop a deeper understanding of their own feelings and the importance of empathy in both human and robot interactions.

Module 03: Tech Explorers – AI, IoT & Cloud in Real Life

Discover how smart homes, wearables, and digital assistants work using concepts of AI, IoT, and cloud computing. Activities: IoT simulations, cloud demos, voice assistants Course Duration: 8 hours


Course Overview:
The Tech Explorers course is designed to provide a fun, hands-on introduction to the exciting world of technology, including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Cloud Computing, Internet of Things (IoT), and Smart Devices. Through interactive activities, real-life examples, and simple explanations, kids will explore how these technologies work and how they
impact our daily lives. This course aims to spark curiosity in young learners and encourage them to think about the future of technology.

Module 04: AI for Kids – Understand & Play with Artificial Intelligence

Learn how machines think, play with machine learning experiments, and see AI in daily life through guided activities.
Activities: Teachable Machine, ML games, ethical AI discussions
Course Duration: 8 hours


Course Overview:
The AI for Kids introduces young learners to the fascinating world of Artificial Intelligence in a fun, hands-on way. Through playful experiments, machine learning games, and real-world examples, kids will learn how AI works and how it’s used in their everyday lives. The course> is designed to engage kids in exploring AI concepts through interactive activities and simple explanations, sparking curiosity and creativity in technology.

Module 05: Digital Creators – Design, Edit, Animate

Master the basics of design and digital creation with Canva, Clipchamp, and other easy tools. Learn to edit videos, make animations, and create presentations.
Activities: Poster design, mini video project, basic animation
Course Duration: 8 hours


Course Overview:
The Digital Creators course introduces participants to the basics of graphic design, video editing, and animation using beginner-friendly tools like Canva, Clipchamp, and other similar platforms. This hands-on course will equip students with the skills to create professional-looking designs, videos, and animations for personal or business use. It’s perfect for beginners looking to dive into digital content creation.

Module 06: STEM in Daily Life – Innovate with Impact

Use technology to solve problems around you. Build mini-projects that mix creativity with real-world relevance.
Activities: Energy-saving model, health-tech idea pitch, social innovation project
Course Duration: 8 hours


Course Overview:
The STEM in Daily Life course introduces participants to the practical applications of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) in solving real-world problems. Through hands-on mini-projects, students will explore how simple technologies can be used creatively to improve everyday life and address social challenges. The course emphasizes innovation, critical thinking, and making a positive social impact using STEM principles.
