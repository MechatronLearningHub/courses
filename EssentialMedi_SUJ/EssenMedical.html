Essential Medical Program

Essential Life Skills Program for Class 5+ by Mechatron Learning Hub

Health & Well-Being Awareness is a practical life skills course designed by Mechatron Learning Hub for students of Class 5 and above. Focused on physical health, safety, and early decision-making, this course empowers students with the awareness and confidence to make smart, healthy choices in everyday life.

Program Snapshot

  • Target Group: Class 5 to 10
  • Offered By: Mechatron Learning Hub
  • Module: 3 sessions (each 90-120 minutes)
  • Format:Interactive classroom/online sessions with real-life simulations
  • Certificate: Life Skills & Health Awareness Certificate
  • Ideal For:Equip students with essential safety, health, and awareness tools

What are you going to learn?

Module 1: Know Your Medicines – Understanding Safe Usage

Students explore exciting future careers through interactive role-plays, games, and virtual job stations.


Activities: Career simulations, interest mapping, day-in-the-life videos
Course Duration: 90 Minutes


Module 2: Drug Avoider Program – Say No the Smart Way

Designed with age-appropriate content, this session builds early awareness about substance abuse, peer pressure, and making positive life choices through stories, visuals, and guided discussions.


Activities: Peer pressure scenarios, decision-making games, story-based learning.

Module 03: First Aid for Juniors – Be Ready to Help

Empower your child with basic first aid skills for everyday injuries like cuts, burns, bruises, or nosebleeds. Learn calm response techniques and emergency actions that could save a life.


Activities: First aid demo kits, emergency response games, safety quizzes
